Frequently Asked Questions:


Q: What is an expense tracker, and why do I need one?

A: An expense tracker helps you keep track of your expenses and categorizes them to help you manage your money how you wish.

Q: How does an expense tracker work?

A: You input any expenses youd like ranging from coffee to car payments and the app will keep track of the expense type and categorize your expenses for you. You can then use the information to better allocate expenses and achieve your financial goals.

Q: Is my financial information safe with this expense tracker?

A: Yes! Firstly, we do not ask for any information that would compromise your bank information like account or routing numbers. The only information we use for expense tracking is the category and the amount. We do use account security though with login credentials using username and password protection.

Q: Is my account accessible from different devices?

A: Yes! As long as you have an internet connection you should be able to login to your account and see your information no matter the device or location.

Q: Is there a mobile app available for this expense tracker?

A: There is not currently a mobile app available, but development is planned for the future.

Q: Can I import debit/credit transactions from my bank account?

A: We do not currently support direct access to transactions through your bank account. All expenses must be recorded manually.

Q: How does the expense tracker categorize expenses?

A: When an expense is entered into the system, the user is asked to pick from a list of transaction types that will be associated with said transaction.

Q: Can I set and manage a budget within this expense tracker?

A: We do not currently support budgeting but plan on implementing in the future.

Q: Does the expense tracker generate reports?

A: The expense tracker does not currently generate reports but it can be added in the future.

Is customer support available if I need help?

A: We do not currently have a customer service team, but we can add a ticket system in the future.
